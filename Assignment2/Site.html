Goals

To create a page to spread awareness regarding the Townsville Community Music Centre to gain new mmbers and give artists a platform to showcase their talent. It will also help the people interested in attending concerts buy tickets online.

Success Evaluation

The success of the site can be evaluated by the number of membership applications recieved after the initialization of the site and the number of tickets sold online. There is also a form for the people interested in giving an auditon to get a chance to perform a the Townsville Civic Theatre

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is anyone who is interested in going to concerts or performing in one. There is no age limit for this site.
The site is designed in a way to grab the attention of anyone interested in attending concerts or anyone interseted in becoming a member of a well know community
